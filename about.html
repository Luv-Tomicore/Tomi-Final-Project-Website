About Us

At TRUESKIN, we believe that true beauty starts with embracing and loving your natural skin.
Founded by Tinuola Odugbesan, a 21-year-old currently living in Switzerland, our brand was created with the heartfelt mission of encouraging people of all skin tones to feel comfortable, confident, and free from insecurities.
Our slogan, “Embrace the True You,” reflects our passion for helping you nourish and care for your skin while celebrating and exploring your unique beauty.
